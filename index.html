Cycling Buddy SG — your Singapore PCN companion

A cycling map of Singapore's Park Connector Network on a real street basemap. Find your position, see the nearest connector, and record your ride — installable and offline-ready.

How to use

  • Drag to pan, pinch/scroll to zoom, tap a coloured path for its loop & park.
  • ◎ Locate — live position + the nearest connector.
  • ⦚ Route — tap a start & destination for offline turn-by-turn that prefers park connectors.
  • ● Record — traces your ride with distance, time and speed; export GPX.
  • Toggle any loop in the Loops panel; tap its ⤢ to frame it.
  • Install it (browser menu → Add to Home Screen) to use it like a native app.

Data & maps
Park connectors (primary): NParks Park Connector Loop (data.gov.sg) — — km, 7 loops. Cycling paths (secondary): LTA Cycling Path Network — — km. Routing & basemap: © OpenStreetMap contributors (ODbL), © CARTO.
